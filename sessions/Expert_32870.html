ROB401-R - [REPEAT] Robotic fleet management with AWS In this chalk talk, learn how companies are using AWS services to build, deploy, and update applications that enable them to manage robots in real time in the field. We review the detailed architecture involved when robots receive orders, navigate their environment, and avoid obstacles in order to choose and retrieve items in a warehouse. In addition, we show you how AWS RoboMaker is used to address one of the bigger challenges for companies—sharing intelligence across robots from different manufacturers—to illustrate a core benefit of an open, cloud-based robotics platform to companies that have a diverse fleet of robots. View Less Chalk Talk Vaibhav Phadnis Vamshi Konduri
