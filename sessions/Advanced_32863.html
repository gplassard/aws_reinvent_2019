GPSTEC306 - Reinventing your technology product strategy with a SaaS delivery model Although this Global Partner Summit chalk talk is open to anyone, it is geared toward current and potential AWS Partner Network Partners. SaaS is a business and delivery model that allows technology providers to monetize their solutions in a frictionless way for customers. Customers want to be able to subscribe to and consume SaaS solutions with ease, and technology providers want to reduce their customers’ time to value. In this chalk talk, learn some of the patterns that we have seen top industry leaders leverage to reinvent their product strategy to deliver their solutions in a SaaS delivery model on AWS.
