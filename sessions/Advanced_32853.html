GPSTEC334 - Architecting a decentralized Ethereum app (DApp) on AWS Although this Global Partner Summit chalk talk is open to anyone, it is geared toward current and potential AWS Partner Network Partners. Despite their reputation for defying conventional deployment paradigms, even blockchain applications require reliable infrastructure, and they make use of several common DevOps patterns at different layers. Indeed, DApps present engineers with challenges that require us to use our ingenuity to maintain high availability and scalability without harming the user experience or sacrificing decentralization. This chalk talk takes you through the process of architecting and developing an Ethereum DApp on AWS, beginning with an architectural overview and covering the development and deployment process with live demos. You get a solid conceptual foundation with which to begin your own blockchain DApp. View Less Chalk Talk Rafael Lopes Carl Youngblood
